    Different types of cologne:
  1. Eau Fraiche
    • 1-3% fragrance oils
    • Lasts for about an hour on skin
    • Much cheaper
  2. Eau de Cologne
    • 2-5% fragrance oils
    • lasts a few hours
    • Also very inexpensive
  3. Eau de Toilette
    • 5-10% fragrance oils
    • Only 1-2 reapplications to make it through the whole day
    • Normally pricier than other options
  4. Eau de Parfum
    • 10-15% fragrance oils
    • Very strong; lasts roughly 5+ hours on skin
    • Slightly more expensive than Eau de Toilette colognes
  5. Pure Perfume
    • 25-40% fragrance oils
    • The strongest cologne that can be found; lasts an entire day on skin
    • Very expensive
